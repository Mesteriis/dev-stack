# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should remain respectful, direct and technically grounded. Disagreement is expected; disrespect is not.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discussing tradeoffs in concrete terms
- giving actionable review feedback
- assuming good faith while still being precise
- keeping communication inclusive and professional

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- personal attacks
- harassment or intimidation
- deliberately hostile or inflammatory language
- sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions that do not meet this standard.

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, issues, pull requests and related communication channels.
